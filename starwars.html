STAR WARS

Star Wars is a science fiction film series that started in 1977 and has constantly continued since. There are seven main movies (so far), an animated movie, two animated TV shows and several future movie releases. The music for every single movie in the series so far has been composed by John Williams, who also composed the music for the first two Jurassic Park movies, the first three Harry Potter movies, all four Indiana Jones movies, the movie Hook, E.T.: The Extra-Terrestrial, and several more.
